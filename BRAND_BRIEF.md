# closr — Brand Brief & Image Prompts

> SaaS chileno para PYMEs que ayuda a **cerrar ventas** (no solo captar leads).
> Doble lectura: "closer" (cerrar ventas + closer de baseball). La `r` huérfana cierra el wordmark.

---

## Brand brief (pegar al inicio de cada prompt para consistencia)

```
BRAND: closr — SaaS chileno para PYMEs que ayuda a CERRAR ventas (no solo
captar leads). Traduce IA/ML a acciones concretas que el dueño de una
panadería, ferretería o ecommerce entiende: "te responde de noche",
"te recuerda hacer seguimiento", "mueve solo los leads listos".

VIBE: Bold accessible, Notion/Atomic style. Flat illustration, geometric,
slightly playful but professional. Latin American warmth, NOT tech-bro,
NOT silicon-valley-startup, NOT corporate.

PALETTE (use exactly):
  primary mustard gold #E5A437
  warm terracotta   #C76A4F
  fresh mint green  #4FD8A3
  cream base        #FAF6EE
  deep charcoal     #1F2024
  electric coral CTA #FF5C39

TYPOGRAPHY: geometric sans, friendly, similar to Plus Jakarta Sans or
DM Sans. Lowercase preferred for the brand name.

DO NOT INCLUDE: robots, holograms, neural network lines, glowing brains,
purple/violet gradients, generic AI clichés, stock-photo handshakes,
floating dashboards in space, code on screens.
```

## 1. LOGO — 3 variantes

### 1A. Wordmark + símbolo (versión principal)

```
Logo design for "closr". Lowercase wordmark in geometric sans-serif,
medium weight, slightly rounded terminals. The letter "o" is replaced
by or overlapped with a speech bubble symbol that has a small mint-green
checkmark inside it — signifying "the conversation that closes the deal".
The "r" at the end has a slightly extended terminal stroke that doubles
as an underline closing the word.

Color: mustard gold #E5A437 wordmark, mint green #4FD8A3 checkmark accent
inside the bubble, on cream background #FAF6EE.

Composition: centered, generous whitespace, isolated mark suitable for
a website header. Vector-clean lines, no gradients, no shadows, no 3D.

Aspect ratio 3:1. High resolution, transparent-background-friendly.
```

```
mustard gold #E5A437, with a clean mint green #4FD8A3 checkmark INSIDE,

Background: cream #FAF6EE inside a softly rounded square (16% corner
radius). No text. No 3D. No drop shadow. Flat vector style, suitable
for both 16x16 favicon and 1024x1024 app icon.

Square aspect ratio. Pixel-clean edges.
```

## Tips de iteración para Nano Banana

1. **Si mete clichés AI** (robots/holograms/violet): agregar al final del prompt:
   `STRICTLY NO robots, NO holograms, NO neural networks, NO purple. Replace any AI symbolism with a simple checkmark or chat bubble.`

2. **Consistencia entre imágenes**: después del primer render que funcione, usarlo como referencia:
   `match the illustration style, palette, and character proportions of the previous image exactly.`

3. **Texto en español dentro de imágenes** es lo más frágil. Si sale mal escrito, dejar `[chat bubble with placeholder text]` y editar después en Figma/Photoshop.

4. **Logo**: los modelos generativos suelen renderizar mal la tipografía. Recomendado:
   - Generar el SÍMBOLO solo con Nano Banana (1B)
   - Crear el WORDMARK en Figma con Plus Jakarta Sans / DM Sans
   - Combinar manualmente

5. **Más variantes**: agregar `generate 4 variations` al final del prompt.
